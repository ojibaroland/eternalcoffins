03. The Sovereign

Our flagship.
In Sapele.

The Sovereign is the most refined piece in our collection — a statement of uncompromising excellence. It is built from quartersawn Sapele, a technique that reveals the wood's extraordinary ribbon grain: a shimmering interlocked pattern that catches light differently from every angle.

The body is finished in a hand-rubbed oil that deepens the grain's natural lustre without obscuring it. Every handle, fitting, and hinge is 24-carat gold-plated — individually polished and inspected. The interior is lined with hand-cut deep burgundy velvet, finished with a sculpted white satin headrest. A piece for someone whose life was, in every sense, remarkable.

Nigerian Hardwood Sapele — The Ribbon Wood

The Wood

Sapele —
ribbon grain.

Sapele (Entandrophragma cylindricum) is native to Nigeria and the wider West African rainforest belt. Its most remarkable quality is its interlocked grain — when quartersawn, this produces the distinctive ribbon figure that alternates between light and dark bands as the viewing angle changes. It is one of the most visually dramatic grain patterns in the world of hardwoods.

Heavier and harder than African Mahogany, Sapele takes fine hand-work exceptionally well. It has been used in the finest Nigerian furniture and interiors for generations. To build The Sovereign in Sapele is to work with the finest timber Nigeria's forests produce.
